How It Works

Step 1: Resume Data Extraction

The system reads and extracts key details from your uploaded resume, analyzing your skills, experience, and job-specific information.

Step 2: Real-Time Question Transcription

Using Vosk Speech Recognition, the app listens and transcribes interview questions in real-time, providing a hands-free experience.

Step 3: Contextual Response Generation

The AI processes the transcribed questions and instantly generates structured responses, displaying them on a teleprompter-style interface.
